FAQ

Questions, answered.

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out and we'll get back to you directly.

How long does a typical project take?+

A single landing page usually takes about a week. A full multi-page site runs 2–4 weeks depending on scope, and e-commerce builds typically take 4–6 weeks.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance after launch?+

Yes — we offer optional monthly maintenance plans covering updates, backups, and small content changes. This is separate from the one-time project fee.

Can I update the website myself after it's built?+

Yes. We can build on a content management system so you can edit text and images yourself, or hand off training so your team can manage it confidently.

What do you need from me to get started?+

Just a sense of your goals, any brand assets you already have (logo, colors), and example sites you like. We'll guide the rest through a kickoff call.

Do you write the content too, or do I provide it?+

Either works. We can write all copy for you as part of the project, or build around content you provide — whichever fits your timeline.

What's your payment structure?+

Typically 50% upfront to begin work and 50% on completion before launch. Larger projects can be split into milestone payments.
